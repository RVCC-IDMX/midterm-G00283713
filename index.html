Wakizashi

George Ghviniashvili

This is my first blade project attempted. I have no sanders or power hammers. Everything was done by hand. The blade is made from unknown carbon steel I had laying around my fathers garage. The dimensions fit this into being a Wakizashi or short Sword. There was much work done in between these 3 images. The hamon is like a Gunome/Choji with many Tobiyaki. The goal is to produce a Hamon in Nie Deki. Blade is yet to be quenched. Still in phase of image 3.
